Minimally invasive surgery relates to any surgical procedure which is performed through tiny incisions instead of a large opening of the body. Smaller incisions have a quicker recovery time and less pain than traditional open operation but with identical benefits as traditional surgery. Some of the commonly practised surgical operations are Laparoscopic surgery, Robotic surgery, and GYN/Pelvic surgery. Our team specializes in General and Minimally Invasive surgical procedures.
